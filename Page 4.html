INFORMATION OF THE SONG

    
    
        
  • Artist: Starship 
    • 
        
  • Album: Nothing's gonna stop us now (no protection) 
    • 
        
  • Release Date: January 19, 1987
    • 
        
  • Songwriter(s): Diane Warren and Albert Hammond 
    • 
        
  • Producer(s): Narada Michael Walden 
    • 
        
  • Inspiration: "Nothing's Gonna Stop Us Now" came from co-writer Albert Hammond's personal life, specifically his impending marriage to his girlfriend after his divorce was finalized. Hammond's statement to Diane Warren, "It's almost like they've stopped me from marrying this woman for seven years, and they haven't succeeded. They're not gonna stop me doing it," directly inspired the song's theme of unwavering love and commitment. The song was also commissioned as the theme for the 1987 romantic comedy film Mannequin.
    • 
        
  • Genre: Rock 
    • 
        
  • Lyrics: "Nothing's Gonna Stop Us Now" describe a powerful, unwavering love between two people who are committed to each other and to building a life together, vowing to overcome any external challenges and setbacks as a united front. The song expresses a deep connection and desire to stay together through thick and thin, achieving their dreams as a strong, resilient team.
